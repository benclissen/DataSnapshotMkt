Local markets are still the most important for National FAs when looking for sponsorship partners (excl. kit suppliers), with the exception of the English FA where over 50% of their sponsors are headquartered outside their home market (no one else has over 20%).

It’s no surprise therefore that Germany, with its economic power and global brands generates the most sponsorship income, although France has the biggest kit deal. It is only England (Emirates, Budweiser) and Germany (Samsung) that have major non-local partners. Despite footballing success, both Spain and Italy have been unable to convert that into major sponsorship deals outside kit deals.
